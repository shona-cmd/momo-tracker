# Privacy Policy for Momo Tracker

**Effective Date: November 28, 2025**

Momo Tracker ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy applies to your use of the Momo Tracker Android app and its integration with GitHub.

## Information We Collect
- **GitHub Data**: Via OAuth, we access public/private repo data (issues, PRs) you authorize. No storage beyond caching for offline use (deleted on uninstall).
- **Device Info**: Android ID, app usage (e.g., tracked issues) for analytics. No personal identifiers.
- **No Sensitive Data**: We don't collect emails, locations, or payments directly—GitHub handles auth/billing.

## How We Use Data
- Enhance tracking (e.g., sync issues).
- Send notifications (opt-out anytime).
- Improve app via aggregated, anonymized stats.

## Sharing
- **GitHub**: For API calls only.
- **Service Providers**: Firebase for push (GDPR-compliant).
- No selling/sharing with third parties.

## Security
OAuth tokens encrypted; data in transit via HTTPS. Follow GitHub's security best practices.

## Your Rights
- Access/delete data via app settings or email support@momotracker.app.
- EU/CA users: GDPR/CCPA compliant—request via email.

Changes? We'll notify via app/update. Questions? support@momotracker.app.

Last Updated: Nov 28, 2025
